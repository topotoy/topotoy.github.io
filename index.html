**Topotoy Games** ![Topotoy Games](/images/topotoy-header.jpg) ABOUT =================================================== Topotoy Games is a tech-driven game initiative based in Madrid, Spain. We are a micro-team of simulation and computer graphics veterans creating unique interactive experiences based on bespoke technology. We understand programming as an artform where the most profound joy lies in achieving a deep understanding of the underlying numerical, mathematical, and physical systems through artisan-level crafting. To achieve this we indulge in the lost art of custom game engine creation, like in the old days.
